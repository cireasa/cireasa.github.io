Piper Configurare Mouse Mmo Manjaro

September 12, 2021 Â· reSH

Am un Logitech G600, mouse MMO (d-ala cu 15 butoane laterale si nu doar)

In Windows am programelul de la Logitech care face treaba fara probleme. In Manjaro am cautat un programel care sa inlocuiasca. Enter Piper .

Instalare super usoara:

1

sudo pacman -Syu piper

Ubuntu:

1

sudo apt install piper -y

Fedora:

1

sudo dfn install piper

Flatpak from Flathub:

1

flatpak install flathub org.freedesktop.Piper
